Our Story

BUILT ON PASSION,
DRIVEN BY PRECISION.

BeamerRepairs was founded in 2009 by Marcus Osei, a former BMW Group master technician who wanted to offer independent drivers the same standard of care they'd expect from an official dealership — without the inflated labour rates and corporate indifference.

What started as a two-bay workshop has grown to a fully-equipped 14-bay facility, staffed by a team of eight certified engineers, each with between five and twenty years of BMW-specific experience. We service everything from a first-car Mini Cooper to a hand-built Rolls-Royce Wraith.
