Warning Signs

SIGNS YOUR BMW MAY NEED ATTENTION

Some issues start subtly. If you notice any of these warning signs, it is worth booking a diagnostic inspection before the problem becomes more serious.

Warning Lights

Dashboard lights such as engine, ABS, or DSC indicators can signal faults that need immediate investigation.

Reduced Power

Loss of acceleration, hesitation, or weak throttle response may point to sensor or fuel system issues.

Leaking Fluids

Oil, coolant, or transmission fluid leaks should be checked quickly to avoid expensive secondary damage.

Electrical Glitches

Random infotainment resets, window faults, or intermittent locking issues often trace back to electrical faults.

Unusual Noises

Grinding, knocking, whining, or rattling sounds can indicate wear in the engine, gearbox, or suspension.

Poor Fuel Economy

A sudden drop in efficiency may be caused by a fault in the engine management, sensors, or emissions systems.
