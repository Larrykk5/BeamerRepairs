What We Offer

EVERY SERVICE.
EVERY SYSTEM.

01

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Full ISTA diagnostic sessions, fault code clearing, engine rebuilds, timing chain, VANOS overhauls, head gasket replacement, and oil system repairs.

02

Annual Servicing

Oil and microfilter service, all-in service (brakes, tyres, wipers, lights), BMW inspection packages, and condition-based service resets.

03

Electrical & Electronics

Battery health and BMS coding, alternator and starter overhaul, ambient lighting, retrofit coding, and full iDrive system diagnosis.

04

Transmission & Drivetrain

ZF 8HP automatic service, SMG / DCT mechatronic repair, manual gearbox rebuilds, differential oil changes, and propshaft vibration diagnosis.

05

Brakes & Suspension

OEM-grade brake pad and disc replacement, EDC shock absorber recalibration, adaptive suspension retrofitting, and 4-wheel alignment.

06

Performance & Tuning

Stage 1 and stage 2 ECU remapping, cold-air intake fitment, downpipe and decat install, plus track-day preparation packages.

07

EV & Hybrid

BMW i3, i4, iX battery diagnostics, high-voltage system checks, PHEV battery balancing, and charge-point fitment advice.

08

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Comprehensive 60-point used-car checks with written report — protect yourself before buying a second-hand BMW or European vehicle.

09

MOT Testing

Class 4 and Class 7 MOT testing on-site. Same-day retests and pre-MOT advisory inspections available Monday to Saturday.
