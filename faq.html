Common Questions

QUESTIONS
ANSWERED.

Will using an independent garage void my BMW warranty?

No. Under EU and UK Block Exemption Regulations, you are free to have your vehicle serviced and repaired at any VAT-registered garage without voiding your manufacturer warranty, provided OEM-specification parts and fluids are used. We always use approved parts and document everything so your service history is fully protected.

Do you use genuine BMW parts?

We use genuine BMW OEM parts as standard. For customers on a tighter budget we can also source OE-equivalent parts from approved suppliers such as Febi Bilstein, Brembo, and Mahle — we'll always discuss your options and won't swap parts without your explicit approval.

How long will my service take?

A standard oil service takes 1–2 hours. An all-in inspection service takes 3–4 hours. Diagnostic sessions vary from 1 hour to a full day depending on complexity. We'll give you a realistic time estimate when you book and update you if anything changes.

Do you offer a collection and delivery service?

Yes — we offer free collection and delivery within 10 miles of our Charlton workshop for services over £250. We also have a fleet of courtesy cars available for loans of up to 72 hours. Both must be booked in advance; please request when making your appointment.

Can you work on non-BMW vehicles?

Yes — we service all vehicles under the BMW Group umbrella (MINI, Rolls-Royce) and have strong capability with Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, Jaguar, and Land Rover. For those marques we use Bosch KTS and Autel MaxiSYS diagnostic platforms.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major debit and credit cards, bank transfer, and cash. We also offer interest-free monthly payment plans through Payl8r for bills over £400 — ask the team for details when you collect your vehicle.

Is my vehicle insured while in your care?

All vehicles in our care are covered by our motor trade insurance policy up to £100,000. A copy of the certificate is available on request. We also have CCTV throughout the workshop and a gated, fenced compound for overnight security.

Do you provide a written quote before starting work?

Always. We send a written quote via email or WhatsApp before any work is authorised. If a diagnostic uncovers additional faults, we pause and contact you for approval — we never proceed without your say-so. No surprises on collection.

Do you offer any kind of guarantee on your work?

Yes — all parts and labour carry a 12-month / 12,000-mile guarantee (whichever comes first). If the same fault returns within that period, we'll investigate and rectify at no charge. Terms are provided in writing with every invoice.

Can I wait at the workshop while my car is serviced?

Yes — we have a comfortable waiting area with Wi-Fi, coffee, and a viewing window into the workshop. For longer jobs we'd recommend a courtesy car or arranging collection, as you'll be more comfortable at home or in the office.

Still have a question? We're happy to help.
